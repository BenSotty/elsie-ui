Transform your genetics workflow with Elsie.

We built Elsie to radically improve patient healthcare by bringing high quality and validated genomic knowledge into everyday clinical practice.

Elsie is a digital health platform to simplify and modernise how genetic tests are accessed, purchased and managed. We bring digital efficiency to streamline the handling of genetic tests, unifying all necessary documentation in a direct and easy-to-use web platform.

We bring individualised medicine to the desktop of every physician. Elsie bridges the gap between genomics knowledge and medical practice, helping physicians make more personalised diagnosis and prognoses adapted to each patient’s genetic makeup.
